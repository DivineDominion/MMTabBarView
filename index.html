MMTabBarView

MMTabBarView is a modernized and view based re-write of PSMTabBarControl.
Though MMTabBarView's API is quite similar, it is no drop-in replacement for PSMTabBarControl. The MMTabBarViewDelegate protocol is somewhat different. But MMTabBarView will help you: The methods of the delegate protocol of PSMTabBarControl have been included and set to deprecated. That means your compiler shows deprecation warnings (if switched on) for all your old delegate method implementations.
Transition will be a matter of minutes.
